About Us

What is Tech4Girls?

Tech4Girls LMS is a learning platform designed to empower Tech4Girls student through technology education. It provides a centralized space where educators can deliver courses and support learners on their journey.

We help bridge the gap between skills and opportunity in the digital world, one student at a time.
